INSPIRING, SHAPING, CREATING: AUDIO-ART@SENNHEISER

This is how the future is made: With its „Future Audio Artist Program“, Sennheiser is making the dreams of young sound artists come true. Future audio leaders are being granted an unprecedented opportuntiy to evolve. What does the program look like? How can you apply? What is the jury looking for?

PROMOTING SOUND ART – WITH €50,000 IN AUDIO EQUIPMENT

Shape the Future of Audio is being applied to art. Those artists selected will be able to use their creativity to explore new frontiers, try out new directions and pursue their visions. Once accepted into the program, they are given carte blanche to creatively express their freedom. Each year, three artists will be sponsored in the creation of one installation each. Sennheiser will support the sound artists by providing €50.000 of the most cutting-edge audio equipment available, thereby making it possible for the artists to focus their originality and creativity in the creation of trend-setting works of sound art.

A NEW DIMENSION OF SOUND

The program was launched at Art Basel in Hong Kong. Artists have until the end of September 2016 to apply for the program. The best sound installations will be premiered for the first time at Art Basel in Miami. In addition, a project by the Swiss artist Zimoun will be presented at this year's Art Basel in Basel.A jury compromised of art-lover and Sennheiser CEO Daniel Sennheiser, renowned curator Prof. Dr. h.c. Walter Smerling and the famous collector Daniela Hinrichs will judge the sound installations and select one program winner at each Art Basel. What is the goal of the "Future Audio Artist Program" and what is the jury looking for when choosing a project to support? We asked the three jury members three questions each about the program.

DANIEL SENNHEISER SPEAKS ABOUT THE BASIC IDEA BEHIND THE PROGRAM

Why did Sennheiser launch this art program? Daniel Sennheiser: "Our mission is to shape the future of audio. We have been doing exactly that for over 70 years. The artistic avant-garde has always been an important driving force. It is where new materials and technologies are often tested and applied long before their industrial implementation. The artistic approach - on the physical and thematic level, free of functional or economic constraints - often shows us new paths that can serve as inspiration for our designers and engineers to go in new directions."
